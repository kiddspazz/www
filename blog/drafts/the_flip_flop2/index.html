Here's a D Flip-Flop diagram:

What the hell does all that mean? Yeah, that's what I thought. ☺

Actually, it's not so bad. The squares with a rounded side are AND gates, the tiny little circles at their rounded tip is an inverter (changes the output from 1 to 0 or 0 to 1). "D" is the input, "Q" is the output, "Q" is the opposite of the output.
